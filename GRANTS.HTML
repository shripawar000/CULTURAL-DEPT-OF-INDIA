Supporting Art, Culture & Heritage

Empowering institutions, artists, and organizations through government grants and funding initiatives.

Available Grants & Schemes

Scheme for Financial Assistance for Promotion of Art and Culture

Provides financial support to NGOs and cultural organizations for organizing cultural activities, workshops, and exhibitions.

National Culture Fund (NCF)

Encourages partnerships with private and public organizations for preserving and promoting India’s cultural heritage.

Fellowships for Artists and Scholars

Offers fellowships to artists, researchers, and cultural workers to promote innovative cultural projects and studies.

Museum Grant Scheme

Supports the development and modernization of museums to improve visitor experience and collection preservation.
