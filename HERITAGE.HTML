About Heritage Conservation

The Ministry of Culture, Government of India, is committed to preserving and promoting India’s diverse heritage. Through initiatives focused on conservation, awareness, and restoration, we ensure that India’s historical legacy continues to inspire generations.

Monuments

Protecting iconic structures such as temples, forts, and ancient ruins that tell the story of India’s glorious past.

Art & Sculpture

Promoting traditional art forms, sculpture, and craftsmanship that represent India’s creative heritage.

Festivals & Culture

Celebrating cultural festivals and practices that unite communities and showcase India’s living traditions.
